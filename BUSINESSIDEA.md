Business Idea: Clearline One-line pitch: The intelligence layer for political prediction markets — decoding whether odds movements reflect real signal or just noise. Problem Prediction market odds are increasingly cited by journalists, analysts, and campaigns as meaningful indicators. But a probability shift from 42% to 51% tells you nothing about why it moved or whether you should trust it. A single whale dropping $200k into a thin market looks identical to a broad consensus shift. Right now, nobody is systematically answering the question: "should I actually believe this number?" Solution Clearline monitors Polymarket (and eventually other prediction markets) and automatically diagnoses every significant market move. For each move, it produces a signal confidence rating backed by on-chain behavioral analysis. It pairs this analytical engine with a public-facing news feed that turns the most interesting findings into short, digestible stories. Two products, one engine: Clearline Dashboard (the platform) — real-time signal ratings, wallet-level breakdowns, alerting, and historical trader accuracy tracking for paying subscribers. Clearline Daily (the media arm) — a free newsletter, Twitter feed, and website publishing 3–5 interpreted market stories per day, driving audience to the paid product. How it works Every flagged market move gets a diagnostic that covers: volume concentration (how many wallets drove it), liquidity context (was the book thin enough for small capital to move price), trader track record (have these wallets been accurate before), cross-market correlation (did related markets move too), and external catalyst matching (was there a polling release, news event, or filing that explains the move). These inputs produce a confidence score: high, medium, or low reliability. Revenue model Three streams: Subscriptions — Free tier gets signal ratings and headlines. Pro tier ($30–50/month) gets full diagnostics, wallet tracking, custom alerts, and historical data. Institutional tier (custom pricing) for newsrooms, campaigns, and research orgs wanting API access or white-labeled embeds. Embeddable confidence widget — News outlets embedding Polymarket odds can license a "confidence badge" that shows the signal rating alongside the probability. This is both revenue and distribution. Sponsorship/advertising on the media side — The newsletter and Twitter feed build audience quickly. Once you have a politically engaged, data-savvy readership, that's valuable sponsorship inventory (think: data platforms, polling firms, political media). Why now Polymarket did over $3.5 billion in volume during the 2024 election cycle. Political prediction markets are no longer niche — they're part of mainstream political coverage. But the analytical infrastructure around them is basically zero. There's no Bloomberg terminal for this space, no Reuters for market-move interpretation. The data is all on-chain and accessible. The window to become the default credibility layer is open right now before the 2026 midterms ramp up. UI Plan: Feature List & User Flows Core features Live Market Feed — scrollable list of active political markets with current odds, recent change, and a color-coded signal confidence badge (green/yellow/red). Users can filter by category (presidential, Senate, gubernatorial, policy) and sort by biggest movers, highest volume, or lowest confidence. Move Diagnostic Panel — clicking any flagged move opens the full breakdown: volume profile chart, wallet concentration breakdown, trader accuracy scores for key participants, liquidity depth at time of move, correlated market movements, and matched external catalysts. The confidence score sits at the top with a plain-English summary sentence. Wallet Tracker — searchable database of anonymized wallets ranked by predictive accuracy, specialization, and lead time. Users can follow specific wallets and get alerts when they enter new positions. Alert System — users configure alerts for: high-confidence moves in markets they care about, specific wallet activity, and large volume spikes. Delivered via email, push notification, or Discord webhook. News Feed (Clearline Daily) — integrated feed of editorially written stories interpreting the day's most notable moves. Each story links back to the relevant diagnostic panel for users who want to dig deeper. Historical Accuracy Tracker — retroactive analysis showing how signal confidence ratings correlated with actual outcomes. This builds trust in the methodology over time. Key user flows Flow 1: "What just happened?" User sees odds shift on Twitter → opens Clearline → finds the market in the live feed → sees yellow confidence badge → opens diagnostic → reads that 78% of volume came from 2 wallets with no external catalyst → concludes the move is likely noise → shares the diagnostic link on Twitter. Flow 2: "Alert me when something real happens" User sets up alerts for all presidential swing state markets with high-confidence moves only → receives push notification that Michigan shifted 6 points on broad-based buying correlated with a new polling release → opens full diagnostic → uses the insight in their analysis or reporting. Flow 3: "Who should I watch?" User browses the wallet leaderboard → finds a wallet with 71% accuracy across 50+ political markets that specializes in Senate races → follows the wallet → gets notified when it takes a new position before the market moves. Flow 4: Casual reader User subscribes to the free newsletter → receives 3–5 short stories daily explaining what moved and why → clicks through on one that interests them → hits the paywall on the full diagnostic → converts to pro subscriber.
